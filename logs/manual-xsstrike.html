Field Manual — XSStrike

XSS validation notes for authorized testing: evidence capture and remediation outcomes.

Scope & safety

  • Only test owned/authorized applications.
  • Avoid disruptive payloads; use minimal validation signals.
  • Prefer staging environments for aggressive testing.

Validation approach (high level)

  • Identify reflection points and sink contexts (HTML, attribute, JS, URL).
  • Confirm encoding/escaping behavior and CSP posture.
  • Capture evidence: request/response and rendered behavior (screenshots/logs).

Reinforcement

  • Context-aware output encoding (correct escaping per sink)
  • Adopt templating frameworks that auto-escape
  • Harden CSP (nonces/hashes), reduce inline script exposure
  • Sanitize untrusted HTML (allowlist-based) where required

Public manual omits payload recipes and bypass techniques. Keep those in private, engagement-scoped documentation.
