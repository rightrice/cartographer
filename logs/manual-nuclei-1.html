Field Manual — 1.nuclei

Advanced notes: reducing false positives, writing templates, and producing defensible findings.

Verification workflow

  • Validate each hit manually or with a secondary method.
  • Capture evidence that stands alone (headers, body snippet, status, timestamps).
  • Classify: informational vs misconfig vs vuln; prioritize by impact.

Template authoring (defensive)

  • Document the assumption the template tests.
  • Make match conditions strict enough to avoid noise.
  • Record template ID and commit hash for reproducibility.

Reinforcement

  • Patch/update affected components; remove exposed admin surfaces.
  • Harden headers, TLS posture, and access controls.
  • Add monitoring around recurring findings.

Public manual omits operational scanning recipes and exploit-adjacent templates.
