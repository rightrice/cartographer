Field Manual — whois

Ownership and registration metadata for domains and IP ranges.

Use cases

  • Confirm registrar, nameservers, and registration dates.
  • Identify abuse contacts and reporting channels.
  • Map IP ownership (ASN/org) during infrastructure analysis.

Safe examples

whois example.com
whois 203.0.113.10

What to record (evidence)

  • Registrar + nameservers
  • Creation/updated/expiry timestamps
  • Org/ASN for IP ranges

Notes

  • Privacy services may redact registrant details.
  • Different registries format output differently.
