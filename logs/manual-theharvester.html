Field Manual — theHarvester

OSINT collection notes: passive-first, source-tagged, and report-ready.

Scope & ethics

  • Use only for authorized reconnaissance or owned assets.
  • Respect rate limits and platform terms of service.
  • Store raw data privately; publish sanitized summaries.

Evidence model

  • Every finding gets: source, timestamp, query, confidence.
  • Correlate discovered emails/domains with known asset inventory.

Reinforcement outcomes

  • Reduce exposed identifiers where possible (public-facing emails, metadata).
  • Improve monitoring for spoofing/phishing targeting.
  • Validate domain portfolio and remove abandoned properties.

Public manual omits operational flags/recipes. Keep exact runs in private playbooks.
